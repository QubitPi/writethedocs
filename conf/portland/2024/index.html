What is Write the Docs?

Write the Docs is a global community of people who care about documentation: Programmers, Tech Writers, Customer Support, Designers, Project Managers, Developer Advocates, and anyone else who wants people to have a great experience with software.

Created in 2013 in Portland, Oregon, WTD has hosted conferences around the world in Prague, Sydney, Amsterdam, London, and Melbourne.

We invite you to our 12th Portland conference to join 400 other attendees to explore the art and science of documentation! Tickets go on sale December 2023.

Interested in being a speaker? We’re accepting Call for Proposals until January 15, 2024.

Schedule Overview

Hike

April 13

If you're in town early, join us for the hike. Portland has excellent access to the woods right in the city.

Writing Day & Reception

April 14

Kick off the conference with Writing Day and work with fellow documentarians on a project. Followed by the opening reception.

Speaker talks

April 15-16

The main days of the conference. Listen to a diverse panel of speakers share their insights and experience. Currently accepting Call for Proposals.

Unconference

April 15-16

Share ideas, solve problems and participate in discussions. Anyone can suggest, lead and attend an Unconference.

Lightning Talks

April 15-16

A Lightning Talk is a short talk where you share an idea or concept. Great for first time speakers, sign ups occur on-site.

Sponsor Expo

April 16

Engage with our Sponsors, discuss job opportunities and interact with new products.
