Schedule¶

Write the Docs is more than a conference. Each year we organize a wide range of events so that people can come together, collaborate, and learn from each other in different ways.

All times are in AEDT.

  • Thursday, December 7
  • Friday, December 8

Thursday, December 7¶

This is the main event! Hear from lots of interesting folks about all things documentation. We will have talks all day, and unconference sessions running in parallel.

Morning tea and lunch will be provided, as well as snacks and drinks all day.

Social Event¶

The official Write the Docs social! Expect a relaxed atmosphere where you can chat and network with your fellow documentarians.
