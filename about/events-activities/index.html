Conferences¶

Our conferences create a time and a place for the global community of documentarians to share information, discuss ideas, and work together to improve the art and science of documentation.

2024 Conferences¶

  • Write the Docs Portland 2024, April 14-16, Portland, Oregon, United States

2023 Conferences¶

  • Write the Docs Portland 2023, May 7-9, Portland, Oregon, United States
  • Write the Docs Atlantic 2023, September 10-12, Virtual - CEST and EDT
  • Write the Docs Australia 2023, December 7-8, Melbourne, Australia

2022 Conferences¶

  • Write the Docs Portland 2022, May 22-24, Virtual - PST
  • Write the Docs Prague 2022, September 11-13, Virtual - CET
  • Write the Docs Australia 2022, December 8-9, Virtual - AEDT

To see the whole list of conferences, see Conferences

Meetups¶

We have meetups in multiple cities every month. To find a meetup near you, take a look at the Meetups page.

If you want to start a new meetup, our Starting a Meetup page has the information that you need.

Sponsorship¶

Find ways to connect with our community on the Community Sponsorship pages of our site. Pick a sponsorship level and perks that suit you. We would love your help in supporting our community.
